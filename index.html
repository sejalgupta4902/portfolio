Sejal Gupta

Sejal is a Masters candidate in the EECS department at MIT. She is working with Dr. Michael Cafarella in the Data Systems Group at CSAIL to explore scientific plot extraction to assist VQA tasks. Beyond her research, Sejal is very interested in the intersection between artificial intelligence and automation in industry. Having worked at Amazon Robotics, NASDAQ, and smaller startups, she loves to work in fast-paced environments. Outside of work, Sejal is a black belt in mixed martial arts and on the path to being a certified yoga instructor.
